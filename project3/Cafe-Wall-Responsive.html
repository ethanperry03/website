Bonus Cafe Wall

This is an alternative version of the animated Cafe Wall Illusion on this page. The difference here is that you can responsively change the colors of the tiles and the lines in real time! If you want to simulate the fade in / fade out feature, just pick white squares. Also, if you want to reset the screen, just hit reload to see the starting animation again.
