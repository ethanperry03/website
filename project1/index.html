About Me

My name is Ethan Perry and I am a Junior at Wheaton College majoring in Computer Science and Math. During my time here I hope to focus my studies on Software Engineering and Data Science. I have found a passion for working with large sets of data through classes like Methods of Data Analysis (Math251) and Machine Learning (Math398). I have also enjoyed the taste of software work I have gained through the main course sequence in the CS major. I hope to continue my academic work by potentially pursuing a Master in Data Science through a company who may take me on at entry level part time.

Outside of the classroom and academic setting I really enjoy staying fit and getting outside. I have found a passion in rock climbing this past summer which was a fun new challenge to use my fitness on. I also love going to the gym and lifting has been a passion of mine for about 5 years. At school I am part of club soccer and ultimate frisbee which are great ways to stay active and social as part of a team. I also really enjoying cooking meals for my family or friends and spending quality time at the table.
