Our Story

Welcome to the CATSC - Cafe At The Science Center, the heartbeat of WeeTon College where technology meets taste! As proud BumbleBees navigating the bustling world of academia, Ethan Perry and Jeffrey Stewart saught to bring an easier means of accessing our lovely food! With the CATSC app, you can place an order from the comfort of your dorm on your phone, tablet, or computer! Our limited but carefully curated menu boasts a symphony of tastes, featuring delectable sandwiches that dance with freshness, and soups that warm the soul. Indulge in our new brick oven pizzas or just grab a drink to quench your thirst. Whatever the case may be, our expert staff and cutting edge app will fuel you for finals!

Todays Specials

Mediterranean Delight Pizza:
Our artisanal crust adorned with a rich and savory garlic-infused olive oil base. Topped with layers of succulent grilled chicken, briny Kalamata olives, sweet roasted red peppers, and a sprinkle of crumbled feta cheese.
Harvest Turkey Club:
This mouthwatering sandwich features thinly sliced roasted turkey, crisp bacon, and creamy avocado, perfectly layered between slices of toasted whole-grain bread.
Pecan Bliss Salad:
This exquisite salad is a symphony of flavors and textures, featuring a bed of crisp mixed greens adorned with candied pecans, crumbled goat cheese, and dressed in a delightful balsamic vinaigrette with a touch of honey.
Tropical Breeze Infusion:
Immerse yourself in a wave of exotic flavors as we blend freshly brewed hibiscus tea with a splash of juicy pineapple and a hint of mint. Don't miss the chance to experience this limited-time tropical paradise in a cup.
